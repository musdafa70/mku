About Us

Mount Kenya University (MKU) is a Chartered University committed to offering holistic education.
MKU has embraced the internationalization agenda of higher education.
The University promotes access to higher education by establishing Campuses and Open,
Distance and E-Learning (ODEL) Centres in different geographical areas in East Africa and beyond.
